We use cookies to provide you with an optimal user experience on PUZH.com.

## What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us to:

- save your preferences
- analyze website usage
- personalize content and advertisements

## What Cookies Do We Use?

- **Essential Cookies**:
  These are technically necessary for the website to function (e.g., page navigation, form functionality, language and theme settings).

- **Analytics Cookies** (optional):
  They help us understand how visitors use our site – e.g., via Google Analytics.

- **Marketing Cookies** (optional):
  These enable us to show you relevant advertising – e.g., through Facebook Ads or Google Ads.

## Your Choice

When you first visit our site, you can decide via a banner whether you want to allow non-essential cookies.
You can revoke your decision at any time in your **browser settings** or delete cookies.
